Project Details
This project tackles the challenge of fragmented, siloed reporting across Sales and Finance at AtliQ Hardware. Using Excel Power Pivot, DAX measures, and dimensional data modeling, I built a unified reporting pack that delivers real-time insights across customer performance, product margins, and market targets.

Key deliverables include: a Customer Discount Approval tool with best/realistic/worst case scenarios, a Market Performance vs Target dashboard across 22 countries, and a full Star Schema data model connecting sales, product, customer, market and date dimensions.

The solution reduces manual reporting effort and gives leadership a single source of truth for commercial decisions.
